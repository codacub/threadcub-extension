Welcome to ThreadCub

ThreadCub helps you save and export your AI conversations with ease. Think of it as your personal chat archive with tiny paws and mighty power!

Key Features

  • One-click chat saving directly from any AI conversation
  • Export conversations as JSON files for easy backup
  • Smart formatting that preserves conversation structure
  • Works with Claude.ai, ChatGPT, Gemini, and more
Getting Started: Simply click the "Save this Chat" button when viewing any AI conversation. ThreadCub will capture the entire conversation and download it as a JSON file to your computer.
